About Us

Free Agent Digital is a Web 3.0 / Blockchain / NFT services providing company. We provide customized NFT to brands and individuals. We have a team of the most talented and passionate blockchain developers. We provide NFT solutions for all blockchains like Ethereum, Solana, polygon, Cardano, Binance smart chain (BSC), and many more!

Our users can participate in our NFT drops by either minting our exclusive NFTs or by minting a customized NFT of their own choice.
