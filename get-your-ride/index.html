Have Any Questions?

How do I book a ride with Get Your Ride?

Booking a ride with Get Your Ride is simple and quick. Visit our website, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, select your preferred vehicle type, and confirm your booking by getting in direct contact with our driver.

What cities do we operate?

We provide service in LYON and surrounding cities like GENEVA, GRENOBLE, VIENNE, VALENCE, DIJON. Contact to get more info.

Can I book a ride in advance?

Yes, you can book a ride in advance with Get Your Ride. During the booking process, you have the option to select a future date and time for your ride. This ensures that your ride will be ready when you need it.

What happens if I need to cancel my booking?

If you need to cancel your booking, you can do so by contacting our customer support team. Please refer to our cancellation policy for specific details regarding any applicable fees or refund eligibility.

Are your drivers vetted and licensed?

Absolutely. All our drivers undergo a rigorous vetting process, including background checks and verification of their licenses. We prioritize your safety and ensure that you are in the hands of experienced and professional drivers.

What should I do if I left something in the ride?

If you left an item in one of our rides, please contact our customer support team immediately with details of your ride and the item. We will do our best to assist you in recovering your lost belongings.
